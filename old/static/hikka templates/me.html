Welcome to HQ


What is Hikkahq?

Firstly - it's a home for so rare in the digital world - anonymity.
This site does not collect your personal information, does not require to verify the identity, and does not store your ip address in its database.
The main rule of the team is to provide means of anonymous communication, without using your IDs on the World Wide Web.

Fortunately, or unfortunately - the site selection policy restricts unauthorized users access to all materials, and forces them to finish their experience using only the authorization page.

We are closed from the eyes of others, a community that wants to maintain the phenomenon of anonymity in the most true form.
